Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add items to my closet?

Go to the Add tab, enter item details (category, color, brand, etc.), then save. Your item will appear in your Closet.

How do I build an outfit?

Use the outfit builder to select a top, bottom, shoes, and any layers. Save the look to reuse it later.

Can I edit or delete items?

Yes. Open an item’s details page and choose Edit or Delete.

Where is my data stored?

Curate stores your closet and outfits in a cloud database. We do not sell your data.

My app feels slow or visuals lag — what can I try?
  • Close and relaunch the app
  • Ensure you’re on the latest version of iOS
  • Reduce very large images (if you add photos)
  • Restart your device
How do I report a bug?

Email support with:

  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Steps to reproduce (if possible)
  • Your device model + iOS version
